# Privacy

The product privacy policy lives at [`docs/legal/privacy-policy.md`](docs/legal/privacy-policy.md).

Key defaults for Epic 13:

- Local-first; no account required for the opening campaign
- Analytics and crash reporting **off** by default
- Local export and deletion available in Settings
- Cloud deletion via `DELETE /v1/progress` and `DELETE /v1/profiles/me`
- Online execution transmits source only when the player runs an online mission
